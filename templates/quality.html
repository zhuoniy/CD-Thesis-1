Quality: About This Data Set

Generally, the data is over-medium quality, with some data missing, but correct and coherent.

Is the data complete?

The data should be complete, because I use the mature tool - AWARE to get data of my phone usage. But as you can see, there are not records on 2018/10/12. All data of this day is missing. One possiblity is that I powered off my phone on the whole day, but it looks little possible and I can't confirm it.

Is the data coherent?

It's coherent. Location is recorded as latitude and longitude, in double float. Screen usage is saved as "2=locked", "3=unlocked", in integers. And battery level is scaled to 100% percentage, in integers.

Is the data correct?

Yes, they are correct. Because all the location records are locations I visited in these days. And I also checked the realtime data of screen usage and battery level, in the collecting period.
